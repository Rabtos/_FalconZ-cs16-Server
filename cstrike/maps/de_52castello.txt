Spanish Castello
----------------------------------------

Setting: Terrorists intruded into an old
spanish castello and try to bomb one of the 
touristic sites.

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent Terrorists
from bombing the bomb targets.  
Team members must defuse any 
bombs that threaten targeted areas.

Terrorists: The Terrorist carrying the
C4 must destroy one of the targets.

Other Notes: There are 2 targets and
30 player-starts in this mission.
