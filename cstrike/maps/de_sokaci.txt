Background:

UN forces are stocking up on food and other
resources to help the people in Angola after
a terrorist attack on the country.

Terrorists:

Bomb one of the two bombsites containing
UN food and resources. Make sure the 
Counter-Terrorists don´t defuse any bombs
that are planted.


Counter-Terrorists:

Prevent the Terrorists from bombing
any of the two locations. If the terrorists
plant a bomb see to it that it is defused
before making any harm.

Note:

There are two bombsites in this mission.
